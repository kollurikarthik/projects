Emoji Battle Guide

Welcome to the Emoji Battlefield! Here's how to play:

1. Choose your emoji warrior: 🪨 Rock, 📄 Paper, or ✂️ Scissors.

2. The Emoji Bot will select its champion.

3. Victory is determined by these cosmic laws:

  • 🪨 Rock smashes ✂️ Scissors
  • ✂️ Scissors slices 📄 Paper
  • 📄 Paper wraps 🪨 Rock

4. Your Emoji Battle Stats update after each round.

5. May the emoji be with you! 🌟
